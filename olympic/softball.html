Haylie McCleney

Country : United States Of America

Sierra Romero

Country : United States Of America

She was also the two-time All-NPF team from 2017 to 2018, three-time Big Ten Player of the Year between 2013 and 2014 and in 2016, Winner of the 2016 Honda Sports Award, USA softball Collegiate Player of the Year in 2016, the NFCA National Player of the Year in 2016 also.

Jennie Finch

Country : United States Of America

She played for the Arizona Wildcats softball team from 1999 to 2002, where she won the 2001 Women's College World Series and was named collegiate All-American. Later she led the United States women's national softball team to the gold medal at the 2004 Summer Olympics and the silver medal at the 2008 Summer Olympics.

Jocelyn Alo

Country : Hawaii

Some of the awards she has won in her career include the two times Women's College World Series in 2021 and 2022, Most Outstanding player in the Women's College World Series in 2022, NFCA National Freshman of the Year in 2018, two-time USA Softball Collegiate Player of the Year in 2021 and 2022.

Rachel Garcia

Country : United States Of America

She also won the 2018 WBSC Women's World Championship, a gold medal in the 2019 Japan Cup, a gold in the 2019 Pan American Games, and a 2018 USA Softball International Cup winner. She was also named the 2015 Gatorade National Player of the Year.

Andrea Duran

Country : United States Of America

She also won two national championships in 2003 and 2004, and was named to the All-Tournament team in 2006. She won a silver medal at the 2008 Summer Olympics.On the Olympic team she played third base and outfield.

Jordy Bahl

Country : United States Of America

Jordyn Bahl (born July 24, 2002) is an American college softball pitcher for Nebraska. As a freshman at Oklahoma in 2022, she was named NFCA National Freshman of the Year.

Danielle Williams

Country : United States Of America

Danielle Lauren Williams is an American college softball player for the Northwestern Wildcats. As a freshman in 2019, she was named NFCA National Freshman of the Year.
