What is Wrestling?

Wrestling is a popular combat sport that involves two competitors grappling with each other in an attempt to gain control and pin their opponent to the ground or force them into submission. It is one of the oldest sports in human history, with roots dating back to ancient times.




By whom, where and when was Wrestling invented?

The origins of wrestling can be traced back to ancient civilizations, making it difficult to pinpoint a specific individual, time, or location where the sport was first invented. Wrestling is one of the oldest forms of combat and physical competition, and its history spans across different cultures.
